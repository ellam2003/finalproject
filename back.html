What are the back muscle group?

The three deep muscles of the back include the semispinalis, multifidus, and rotatores. These muscles stabilize the vertebral column and play a role in proprioception and balance.
Moreover, these muscles help with the movements of the vertebral column and maintain posture.

Source: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK537074/
