What are triceps muscle

The triceps brachii is a large, thick muscle on the dorsal part of the upper arm.
It is composed of three heads (tri = three, cep = head): a long head, a lateral head, and a medial head

Source: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK536996/
