What are the abs muscle group?

This muscle group consists of 4 main muscles: Transversus abdominis, Rectus abdominis, and the external and internal oblique muscles.
The most well known muscle in this group is Rectus abdominis, It is a paired muscle, separated by a midline band of connective tissue called the linea alba.
